SILENCE FORÊT is a collective of artists bringing together Louise Wang, Caroline Savary, Nathan Solioz,
Tamara Liardet, Tiki Bordin and Mathilde Fenoll. Young students at the Geneva School of Art and Design
and divided into various sections (interior architecture, visual arts, jewelry design ..) we draw our
strength from the diversity of our backgrounds and our training within the Head.
Established in 2015, Silence Forêt is a nomadic collective welcoming and setting up projects such as group
exhibitions, fashion experiences, artists books, zines and other prints.
Since September 2018 the collective has moved to Geneva and focuses on a work of art installations and
performances mixing formats and mediums, still focused on collective projects.
This new geography is driving a burgeoning energy. On this web platform and between the shores of Lake
Geneva, will spread very soon our future ordinary obsessions.
